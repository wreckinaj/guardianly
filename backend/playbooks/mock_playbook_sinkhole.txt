HAZARD: Sinkhole / Roadway Collapse.
Context: Sudden localized collapse of the ground or street. High risk of secondary widening and compromised underground utilities (gas, water).
Recomm: Maintain a wide berth. The ground surrounding the visible perimeter may be hollow and severely unstable.
Action: Stop and reverse direction immediately. Do not attempt to walk or drive near the edge. Report the exact location if authorities are not yet present.
