HAZARD: Flooded Pathway / Flash Flood.
Context: Standing or moving water covers the walking path. Water depth is deceptive and often hides open manholes, downed power lines, or sharp debris.
Recomm: Never walk through moving water. Just six inches of fast-moving water can knock an adult off their feet.
Action: Turn around and move to higher ground immediately. Reroute your walk to completely avoid the flooded zone.
