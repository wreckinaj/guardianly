HAZARD: Low Visibility (Fog/Smoke/Heavy Spray).
Context: Forward visibility is severely reduced, making it difficult to spot oncoming hazards.
Recomm: Turn on low-beam headlights immediately. Do NOT use high beams, as the light will reflect back and worsen visibility.
Action: Use the right edge of the road or the painted fog line as a guide to stay in your lane. Do not stop on the active roadway.
