HAZARD: Extreme Heat Warning.
Context: Dangerously high temperatures increasing the risk of heatstroke and rapid dehydration, especially for pedestrians and cyclists.
Recomm: Seek air-conditioned shelter immediately. Limit all strenuous outdoor activity.
Action: Locate the nearest cooling center, library, or public indoor space (Mapbox POI search). Drink water even if you do not feel thirsty.
