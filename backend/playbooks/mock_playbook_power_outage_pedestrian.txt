HAZARD: Power Outage (Pedestrian Focus).
Context: Streetlights and crosswalk signals are completely disabled. Pedestrians are nearly invisible to drivers, and intersections are chaotic.
Recomm: Do not assume vehicles will stop or that drivers can see you. Treat every crossing as extremely hazardous.
Action: Turn on your phone's flashlight to make yourself visible. Stay strictly on sidewalks, avoid unlit alleyways, and only cross streets when absolutely clear.
